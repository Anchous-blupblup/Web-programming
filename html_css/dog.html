Warm paws, cold snow


"Warm paws, cold snow" is an exciting game that will take you into the world of north's fairy tales. In the game you will meet mysterious characters who will tell you entertaining stories about northern people's traditions and fairy tales. You will learn to manage your dogs, feed them and take care of them so that they are always ready to help you in difficult times.
